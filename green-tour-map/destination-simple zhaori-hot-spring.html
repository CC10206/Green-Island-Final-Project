Zhaori Hot Spring

朝日溫泉

Located on the southeastern coast of Green Island, Zhaori Hot Spring is a world-class natural wonder and one of only three known saltwater submarine hot springs in the world. Heated by the island's volcanic geology and fed by the Pacific tides, this rare geothermal site offers an extraordinary open-air soaking experience right on the coral reef shore.

Submarine Geothermal Soaking

The open-air tide pools fill naturally with geothermal saltwater, allowing visitors to soak in mineral-rich warmth while surrounded by the raw ocean landscape. This therapeutic experience is best enjoyed at low tide after sunset, where the cool night air and the rhythmic sound of the crashing waves create a deeply relaxing, immersive sensory connection with nature.

Greeting the Pacific Sunrise

True to its name, which means "morning sun," the hot spring serves as the island's most legendary spot for witnessing the dawn. Soaking in the terraced pools as the first light breaks over the horizon offers a spectacular sunrise panorama, beautifully merging Green Island's unique volcanic heritage with the endless expanse of the Pacific Ocean.
