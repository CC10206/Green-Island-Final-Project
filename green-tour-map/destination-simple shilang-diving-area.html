Shilang Diving Area

石朗潛水區

Located on the western coast near Nanliao, Shilang is celebrated as Green Island’s premier diving and snorkeling sanctuary. Shielded from the strongest Pacific currents, its remarkably calm, crystal-clear waters serve as the ultimate gateway to the island's internationally acclaimed marine life and spectacular underwater topography.

The World-Renowned Underwater Wonders

Shilang is globally famous for its unique submerged landmarks, most notably the deep-sea Underwater Mailbox, which allows divers to mail a real waterproof postcard from beneath the waves. Nearby, the romantic Heart of the Ocean art installation offers an enchanting backdrop, perfectly blending human creativity with the endless blue of the Pacific.

A Haven for Marine Biodiversity

As the island's ultimate diving sanctuary, Shilang's protected reefs harbor an exceptionally rich and flourishing ecosystem. Divers and snorkelers can glide seamlessly through a mesmerizing wonderland of vibrant coral reefs, towering brain corals, and schools of curious tropical fish, offering an unparalleled look at Green Island’s pristine underwater heritage.
