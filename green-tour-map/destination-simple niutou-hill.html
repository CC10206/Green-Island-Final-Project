Niutou Hill · Green Island

Located on the northeastern coast of Green Island, Niutou Hill is a striking volcanic headland named for its remarkable resemblance to a resting ox facing the sea. As one of the island's most iconic landmarks, this high-altitude plateau offers expansive panoramic views and serves as a premier destination for experiencing raw coastal beauty.

The Oceanside Grassland Plateau

The summit of the hill transitions into a surprisingly vast, rolling green grassland that contrasts beautifully with the deep blue of the Pacific. Visitors can safely wander across the open pastures to the edge of the sheer cliffs, experiencing a liberating sense of endless space as the coastal winds sweep across the plateau.

A Panoramic Sunset Vantage

Perched high above the waves, Niutou Hill provides an unobstructed vantage point for capturing the island's dramatic topography. It is a coveted spot for photographers to witness the glowing sunset, framing the volcanic basalt formations and roaring sea below, capturing the timeless, untamed essence of Green Island's geological heritage.
