Our SustainableGoals

This website aligns with UN Sustainable Development Goals11 (Sustainable Cities and Communities)and14 (Life Below Water). By integrating Green Island tourism info, marine ecology insights, and eco-friendly local communities, we aim to deliver a travel experience that is both enjoyable and ecologically responsible.

SDG 11：Sustainable Cities and Communities

By providing local services, eco-friendly accommodations, and low-impact travel recommendations in Green Island, we seamlessly integrate urban and community sustainability into every traveler’s daily itinerary.

SDG 14：Life Below Water

By highlighting Green Island’s ecological crises under global warming and high tourism pressure, we aim to inspire travelers to deeply understand the value of marine resources and actively take part in conservation efforts.
